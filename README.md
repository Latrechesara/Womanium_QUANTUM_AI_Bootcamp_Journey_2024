Contains my notes, quizzes, certificates, reflections, and project materials from the [Womanium Quantum + AI 2024](https://www.womanium.org/Quantum/AI) program.

This repository documents my journey through the bootcamp, from learning the foundations of quantum computing and AI to contributing to an award-winning technical project recognized by the **US Naval Nuclear Laboratory for Best Technical Merit**.

**Note :** This repository is meant to document both my technical learning and my personal growth during the Womanium summer bootcamp experience.

<hr/>

## About the Program!


Womanium Quantum + AI 2024 was an intensive summer program combining:

- Quantum computing foundations
- Artificial intelligence and machine learning
- Hands-on technical learning
- Industry challenge projects
- Global collaboration and mentorship

For me, this program was more than a bootcamp. It was a research-oriented learning journey that strengthened my motivation to pursue a PhD.

## Bootcamp Phase

## QBronze : Introduction to Quantum Computing & Programming

This module documents my work in **QBronze**, part of my Womanium Quantum + AI journey.

QBronze introduced the foundations of quantum computing and programming through a structured bootcamp format. It covered both classical and quantum basics, then moved toward key quantum ideas such as qubits, multi-qubit systems, entanglement, and Grover’s search algorithm.

The bootcamp experience helped me see more clearly that I want to pursue a PhD and continue building at the intersection of research, quantum computing, and AI.
